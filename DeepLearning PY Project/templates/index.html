Driver Drowsiness Detection

Project Overview

The Driver Drowsiness Detection project is a safety-focused system designed to detect and prevent accidents caused by drivers falling asleep at the wheel. The system typically uses a combination of computer vision techniques, machine learning models, and real-time monitoring of the driver's behavior. The project involves detecting signs of drowsiness, such as closed eyes, yawning, or head tilting, using a camera and pre-trained models. Once drowsiness is detected, the system can issue an alert (such as an audible alarm or vibration) to wake the driver and prevent accidents.

Importance of Driver Drowsiness Detection in Our Lives

  • Prevention of Road Accidents
  • Saving Lives
  • Enhancing Road Safety
  • Economic Benefits
  • Support for Commercial Drivers
  • Technological Advancement in Automotive Safety
